AFRAKEN Website Project

Building the Association of France Alumni's website

AFRAKEN is an Association of graduates who completed their studies in France, residing and/or working in Kenya.

Our project focuses on delivering high-quality software solutions that enhance user experience and efficiency. We have a dedicated team working tirelessly to ensure the success of this project, and we are hosted by a prestigious organization that supports our goals and mission.

Prototyping
